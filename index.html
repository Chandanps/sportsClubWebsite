INSPIRATION

The Club gets its root inspiration from of the greatest sportsmen and sportswomen who were as well a pure gentlemen and ladies like Milkha Singh, P T Usha, Usain Bolt, M S Dhoni and many more.

The main aim is to provide the best of the quality coaching to the deserving people with the least possible amount of fee and sometimes no fee at all for the needy.

It's not an easy task to come nothing and nowhere and to reach the highest standards and platforms of sports. We are just a pushing force here and this force is really crucial at least in the beginning stages.
